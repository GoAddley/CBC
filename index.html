About Crystelle

Hi I am Crystelle, but please call me Cris! I am the owner and operator of Carnelian Bella Counselling. I just wanted to share with you how I picked my business name! My passion for working with families started with my own, so therefore it felt appropriate to draw from my family when it came to naming my business. I love working with people from all works of life, however my passion is working with individuals, couples, and families, especially familiesthat are going through different transitions, be it adjusting to a new addition to the family, or struggling to communicate, or those that decide seperation/divorce is the best way for them, or blended families that are trying to find balance and acceptance within their new family situation.

I offer a large amount of empathy and compassion to all my clients no matter their situation, especially as a mother myself, who has blended family in a complex household. Over the past two years I have supported others going through the life trials of seperation/divorce and balancing the additions of new family members. I have sessions available to my clients Monday through to Friday and every second Saturday, I am looking forward to meeting you and of finding ways to better support you and/or your family.

Services

Services

Individual

There are many reasons why people seek out counselling. Yes, a large amount of time it is for a serious resons such as grief, relationships, trauma/abuse, and many other mental health ailments. However, counselling is not a tool that is restricted to those life affecting concerns but also there to learn healthy coping strategies, and a great way to have a self-check in order to assist in not only getting on track but staying on track.

Couples

Seeking counselling as a couple is usually made as a last resort in saving a marriage or relationship. This does not have to be the case, understanding that counselling does NOT mean your relationship is over or at the end. The simple fact that both parties are present is a great step in showing that you both wish to work on your relationship. Learning communication, understanding and trust in a relationship does not magically happen and some couples need a little assistance learning the tools needed to co-exist, and potentially overcome learned behaviours from previous relationships.

Family

Life affecting stressors and changess to the family unit sometimes need a helping hand, and seeking counselling is a tool that many use to assist the family to make the necessay adjustments as smothl as possible. This is an area that I am particularly passionate about especially as a mother myself, who has blended family in a complex household.
