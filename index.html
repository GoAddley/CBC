Meet Crystelle

Hi I am Crystelle, but please call me Cris! I am the owner and operator of Carnelian Bella Counselling. I just wanted to share with you how I picked my business name! My passion for working with families started with my own, so therefore it felt appropriate to draw from my family when it came to naming my business. My daughter and I like to collect rocks and stones and have books to help find out what their names are. There is a stone called Carnelian, and this stone is supposed to promote healing and wellbeing, and not just generally but also mental healing and wellbeing. Bella is a nod to a family heritage of someone that is very close to me. So when you put these two together you get healing and beauty, which makes healing beautifully counselling the underlying message of Carnelian Bella Counselling.
