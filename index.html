About Crystelle

Hi my name is Crystelle, but please call me Cris! I am very excited at the prospect of us working together, it gives me great joy working with people from all walks of life and look forward to hearing your story. Choosing a counsellor to work with can be very daunting, as it takes a great deal of trust to open up to someone about something that is bothering you. With this knowledge I aim to develop meaningful, supportive and authentic relationships with those I work with. As a parent, who has experienced divorce and has a blended family in a complex household, I feel that I am able to relate to many of life's trials. I offer a large amount of empathy and compassion with a person-centred approach, that focuses on working towards solutions based on your strengths. Through my continued study in, child development, effective parenting, relationships and conflict resolution; I have found that the skill set required for these subjects have come naturally to me, which has flared my passion to support those within this area. General counselling aside, my passion is to work with individuals that are going through life transitions. These transitions can be, but not limited to: adjusting to a new addition/s; struggling with communication; the decision to separation/divorce; blended families; co-parenting; etc.Thank you for your time, and for letting me share a little bit about myself, I hope that we get the chance to work together moving forward.

Services

Individuals

There are many reasons why an individual may seek out counselling, and it is nearly always a daunting and/or confronting process. There are many reasons that individuals seek out counselling such as, but not limited to: grief, relationships, trauma, abuse, mental health ailments, etc. Counselling is tool that is not restricted by only life affecting concerns but a way of gaining the necessary healthy tools in coping is stress and building strategies that are based on the individual strengths.

Relationships

Maintaining a relationship is not always easy as pie. It can take a lot of work for both parties to maintain the level of communication and trust that is expect from each other. Learning to communicate with your partner does not always magically happen and some couples need a little assistance in learning the tools needed to co-exist.

Parenting

Parenting is hard. And one size does not fit all. What works for one child may not necessarily work for another. As children grow, so do parents and some days parents are making it up as they go. Counselling can be empowering for a parent that is struggling or worrying about if they are doing it right. Sometimes parents need the reminder that they are not alone, and sometimes it does take a village to help raise the tiny human/s that you have brought into the world.
